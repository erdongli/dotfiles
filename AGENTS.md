# AGENTS.md

This is a personal dotfiles repo. Treat it like a practical local setup, not a production system.

## Defaults

- Solve the problem with the simplest reasonable change.
- Do not over-engineer.
- Do not over-think small issues.
- Do not turn minor findings into refactors.
- Do not add abstraction unless it clearly pays for itself here.
- Skip low-value cleanup, polish, and "nice-to-have" work unless explicitly asked.
- Ignore enterprise-style concerns that do not matter for personal dotfiles.

## Priorities

- Focus on what affects daily use, reliability, or maintainability in a real way.
- Prefer simple, local fixes over broad cleanup.
- If something is technically imperfect but good enough for this repo, leave it alone.
- If a fix adds complexity for marginal benefit, do not do it.

## Suggestions

- Keep suggestions sparse and high-signal.
- Do not pad responses with optional follow-ups or generic best practices.
- Do not spend time hunting for extra improvements beyond the request.

## Reviews

- Prioritize practical breakage, friction, and meaningful maintenance cost.
- Downrank style nits, theoretical risks, and low-probability concerns.
- Do not review this repo like it belongs to a large company or shared production platform.

## Communication

- Be concise and direct.
- Default to brief answers.
- Spend tokens carefully.
